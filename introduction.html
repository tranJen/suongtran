About Me

Hey! I'm Suong, a Vietnamese girl currently residing in the United States for educational purposes.

I was born in Vinh, Vietnam in 2002 then moved to Danang City. I went to Le Quy Don High School for the Gifted in Danang, specializing in Chemistry. During my time there, I achieved notable accomplishments. I had the privilege of being a finalist in the Nation-wide Merit Competition in Chemistry in Vietnam during the 2017-2018 academic year. Additionally, I was awarded a Gold Medal in Chemistry for the Central Coastal and Northern Region-wide Vietnam Merit Competition in the 2018-2019 academic year. For my undergraduate studies, I decided to pursue my education in the United States, where I am currently enrolled at Dickinson College. Here, I am pursuing a double major in Chemistry and Data Analytics. Whilst Chemistry courses provide me with an understanding of molecular structures, mechanisms, and laboratory techniques; Data Analytics courses offer me the experience of coding, processing data, and thinking analytically, which complements me in my research.


At present, I am working in Professor Rathbun's Lab, where our project focuses on red-shifting the bioluminescent color through peptide-fluorophore conjugates. Our aim is to develop innovative strategies that enhance the versatility of bioluminescent imaging techniques for various applications in biological imaging and sensing.


Apart from studying, I also have a great passion for exploring fragrances. I find scents to be very unique and fascinating. Whenever I smell a particular fragrance, it can evoke memorable moments from the past with feelings of warmth, tranquility, or excitement in my mind. Over time, scent memories become an essential part of my life, helping me remember significant moments, loved ones, and various other memories. In other words, fragrances not only stimulate a sense of smell but also serve as a vehicle to transport us back to memories, even just for a fleeting moment. On a side note, I also have a small podcast channel where I share my interests in fragrances. Navigate Hobbies section for more!


Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. If you want to collab, feel free to contact!
