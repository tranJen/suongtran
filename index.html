About Me

I am an undergraduate double majoring in Chemistry and Data Science at Dickinson College. I am working in Professor Rathbun's Lab, where my project focuses on red-shifting the bioluminescent color through peptide-fluorophore conjugates. Our aim is to develop innovative strategies that enhance the versatility of bioluminescent imaging techniques for various applications in biological imaging and sensing. I love walking around with my camera. I also love talking to new people and learning about what they are working on, so feel free to reach out!
